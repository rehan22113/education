Admission Process and Eligibility

  • Eligibility
  • Admissions
  • Fees

Eligibility 1
Bachelor's degree (10+2+3/4) or equivalent qualification in any discipline from a recognized University with a minimum 55% score.
OR
Eligibility 2
Students who have appeared for their final year degree examination can also apply, however, their admission will be provisional and will be confirmed only after producing the results.

If you meet one of these criteria, you are eligible to apply for the programme. However, you will receive a letter of confirmation about your admission only post clearing the entrance test and personal interview round.

Step 1: Application submission
Check your eligibility, pay the fees for the IIDE Admissions Test, and fill in the application form in detail and take the Admissions Test. The test is designed to assess creativity, aptitude, communication, acumen, general knowledge to gauge the applicant’s vision & interest in Digital Marketing.

Step 2: Application review
All applications are thoroughly reviewed by the admissions committee. Shortlisted students will be called for a personal interview during which they can present their portfolios.

Step 3: Personal Interview
Each shortlisted applicant will be personally interviewed by the admissions committee. The committee will assess you on your aptitude, tech-savviness, and general awareness towards Digital Marketing trends and news. You may also be asked to produce proof of educational and professional qualifications during the interview. Qualified applicants will receive a letter confirming the admission to the Post Graduate Programme in Digital Marketing

Q. Is an MBA in Digital Marketing good?

Yes, an MBA in Digital Marketing is an excellent option for all fresh graduates who want to learn digital marketing as well the managerial skills to enter the industry or for working professionals who want to progress in their careers. It is an in-depth study of digital marketing which has emerged as one of the strongest domains in the business world today. Read more about the scope of an MBA in Digital Marketing here.

Q. What are the placement opportunities after this MBA in Digital Marketing – Post Graduation Programme?

Here is a list of the job profiles that you can land for yourself after the completion of this programme: Brand Manager, Client Servicing Manager, Content Marketer, Account Manager, Paid Media Expert, SEO Expert, Business Development Manager, and Media Strategist.

Q. Who can learn MBA in Digital Marketing?

Anyone who has completed their bachelor’s degree, from any field or specialization can learn MBA in Digital Marketing. There is no bar to who can do digital marketing, as long as you have a passion and desire to learn.

Q. What is the salary of a digital marketer?

The average starting digital marketing salaries in India will range from 2 – 4 LPA for candidates who have completed this programme. Depending on the candidate’s experience and skill base, the package can also range between 4-6 LPA. This course prepares you and jumps you up to a mid-senior managerial position in your very first job. For working professionals, a significant salary hike can be expected post-completion of the program.

Q. Does IIDE provide scholarships?

Yes, IIDE provides scholarships up to INR 60,000. A student is eligible for a scholarship in our MBA in Digital Marketing – Post Graduation Programme, if they have scored either 7.5 CGPA or 75% in the last, appeared exam.
If you fulfill the above criteria, you will then need to go through an aptitude and creativity test designed by IIDE. Based on your performance, we shall get back to you regarding your scholarship eligibility. The scholarship, if qualified for, shall be up to 30% of course fees.

Q. What is the fee structure for IIDE’s MBA in Digital Marketing – Post Graduation Programme?

The fee for our MBA in Digital Marketing – Post Graduation Programme is INR 4,00,000 + Taxes. We also offer student loans, flexible installment options, and no-cost EMI starting from INR 39,999/month.

Q. Is there a Batch transfer fee?

Yes, students will have to pay a batch transfer fee and the difference in the tuition fees if any at the time of readmission. Students can apply for a batch transfer within 180 days from the date of the orientation of said batch by writing an email to the course management team at pgpdm@iide.co. To know more, get in touch with our counselors.

Q. Where is IIDE’s campus for MBA in Digital Marketing – Post Graduation Programme in Mumbai?

IIDE’s campus is in Andheri West, Mumbai. This campus is 5 minutes walking distance from the local railway station.

Q. What is the MBA in Digital Marketing – Post Graduation Programme format?

Currently, the programme follows a hybrid format of Offline + Online where students have an option to either come to campus in Andheri, Mumbai, and learn offline or attend the live sessions of the same class from their home. Note: This is subject to change at the discretion of the management.

Q. Does IIDE provide certifications?

Yes, this programme provides you with a certificate of completion from IIDE denoting your mastery of digital marketing. Apart from certifications, we also provide resume-building guidance and interview mentoring. So when you apply for digital marketing jobs, your profile shall gain more attention from employers. By the end of this course, you will be prepared for Google and Facebook level certifications to bolster your resume. The cost of their tests, if any, is to be borne by the student.

Q. Do you have lectures on Saturdays?

Yes! Our batch is from Monday to Friday with certain lectures scheduled on Saturdays; at the discretion of the management.

Q. Does IIDE offer placement assistance after this MBA in Digital Marketing – Post Graduation Programme?

Yes, we provide 100% placement assistance to our students’ post completion of the programme. Our students have been directly placed into India’s top digital marketing agencies and brands through our placement cell. We help you get noticed by top brands in the country and connect you with them to get special attention for you in this highly competitive job market.

Q. Is there an MBA in Digital Marketing?

Yes. Many institutes offer an MBA in Digital Marketing like IIDE which has a comprehensive syllabus that will impart the right Digital Marketing skills and relevant Managerial skills needed to bag a job with India’s leading brands and digital setups.
IIDE’s curriculum is created with consultation from MBA mentors across leading institutes to design a course that captures all the industry-relevant skills that are needed by marketers and managers of today.
